# Setup: Bullish Kill Zone VAH Break — MTF Triple Alignment
**Mirror of:** `2026-04-15_bearish_killzone_val_break`  
**Session:** Any kill zone — London (3–4am ET), NY AM (10–11am ET), NY PM (2–3pm ET), Asian (overnight)  
**Instrument:** COMEX_MINI:MGC1! (Micro Gold Futures) — 1 Minute Chart  

---

## Core Concept

Exact mirror of the bearish VAL break setup. Price breaks above the entire MTF value area (above VAH on all 3 timeframes), pulls back to test VAH from above, fails to break back below, then accelerates into the LVN above.

---

## Market Context Required

| Timeframe | Price Position |
|-----------|----------------|
| 1H        | ABOVE VAH      |
| 15m       | ABOVE VAH      |
| 5m        | ABOVE VAH      |

**All three timeframes: price outside and above value area = full bullish alignment.**

Kill zone volume profile shows POC well below current price, with price having broken above the entire value area. This is the mirror image of the bearish setup — smart money has swept liquidity below session VAL and is now accumulating and driving price up through the value area.

---

## Setup Anatomy

### Phase 1 — Accumulation / First Leg Up
- Large bullish volume bar — first sign of institutional buying
- Spike DOWN below kill zone VAL (below all 3 VALs) then immediate reversal — classic **liquidity sweep below VAL**, smart money takes long-side liquidity
- Multiple consecutive bullish bars with increasing volume
- The largest volume bar confirms accumulation
- Price breaks through 1H VAH, 5m/15m VAH on the way up

### Phase 2 — Failed Pullback / Alert Zone
**This is where the ALERT fires. This is what to watch for.**
- Two to four pullback bars from the VAH area back down toward VAH
- **Pullback volume is LOWER than the buying bars** — no conviction from sellers
- Price cannot close back below 1H VAH or 5m/15m VAH
- Multiple attempts over several bars — each one holds higher lows
- Key tell: **price retesting broken VAH from above and holding**
- The longer it churns above VAH without breaking back below = more bullish

### Phase 3 — Entry Trigger
- Final dip tests 5m/15m VAH from above, closes just above it
- Next bar turns back up
- **ENTRY: Long on break of the pullback high, or on close of the first bullish bar after failed retest**
- **STOP: Below the failed pullback low**
  - Aggressive stop: below nearest swing low = 15–20 tick risk
  - Conservative stop: below all pullback lows = 35–45 tick risk

### Phase 4 — LVN Acceleration
- Clean break resumes the move up
- **LVN above VAH causes price to accelerate sharply** — thin area, no resistance
- High-volume bull bar blasts through LVN zone
- Weak pullback bar on lower volume — no conviction from sellers
- Second wave of buying continues to next VP level

## Entry, Stop, Target

| Parameter | Notes |
|-----------|-------|

| **Entry (conservative)** | Break above the pullback swing high confirmed |
| **Stop (tight)** | Below nearest swing low of the pullback |
| **Stop (standard)** | Below all pullback lows (below every bounce attempt) |
| **Target 1 (min)** | First HVN above entry — minimum 2:1 R:R |
| **Target 2 (full)** | Next session VP level above (POC or VAH of next session) |

**Target framework: value area to value area — nearest session VP level above in direction of trade**

---

## Alert Criteria (When to Get Ready)

Alert fires when ALL of the following are true:
1. Price closes ABOVE VAH on 5m timeframe
2. Price closes ABOVE VAH on 15m timeframe
3. Price closes ABOVE VAH on 1H timeframe
4. We are more than 30 minutes into the kill zone session
5. At least one pullback attempt has failed to break back below VAH (holding above)

**This is the WATCH state — not entry yet. Start monitoring for entry trigger.**

---

## Entry Criteria (When to Pull the Trigger)

Enter LONG when ALL of the following are true after the alert fires:
1. Price attempted to break back below VAH at least once and failed (closes back above)
2. The pullback volume was noticeably lower than the buying volume
3. A bullish bar closes at or near its high after the failed pullback
4. Price is making higher lows on each pullback attempt
5. No major key level (Weekly VH, PDaily VH, Overnight VH) sitting within 20 ticks above as immediate resistance

---

## Take Profit Criteria

**Partial / Target 1:**
- First major HVN above entry, OR
- 5m/15m VAH of the new value area forming above, OR
- Minimum 2:1 R:R from entry

**Full / Target 2:**
- Next key horizontal level (PDaily VH, Overnight VH, Weekly VH), OR
- Price closes back inside value area on 5m (momentum exhausted), OR
- Volume spike on a pullback bar suggesting absorption by sellers

**Trail stop rule:**
- Once Target 1 hit, move stop to breakeven
- Let rest run to Target 2 or until 5m candle closes back below 5m VAL

---

## Stop Management

- Initial stop below the lowest pullback low before entry
- If price immediately accelerates (large volume bull bar through LVN), trail stop to the swing low of the most recent pullback
- Exit immediately if price closes back below 5m/15m VAL — setup is invalidated

---

## Key Observations / Lessons

1. **The liquidity sweep below VAL** (spike down below kill zone VAL then reversal) is the earliest warning sign — smart money swept stops and is now long.

2. **Volume signature on pullbacks is the confirmation**: buying bars should show higher volume than pullback bars. If pullback bars match buying volume, sellers are absorbing — be cautious.

3. **The LVN above kill zone VAH** causes price to accelerate sharply. This is what creates the 3:1 R:R — the target is further away than the stop because price moves fast through thin areas.

4. **Multiple bars of churning above VAH** before the move resumes. Patience required. The alert fires early, the entry is later. Do not rush the entry on the first breakout.

5. **Draw key levels before the session** — Overnight VH, PDaily VH, Weekly VH above the setup will identify your target zones in advance.

---

## What Would Have Invalidated This Setup

- Price closing back BELOW 5m/15m VAL with strong volume = abort
- Price finding a major key level (Overnight VH, PDaily VH) within 20 ticks of entry = wait
- Kill zone less than 30 minutes old = too early, profile not yet reliable
- All three MTF VP pullbacks showing INCREASING volume = sellers absorbing, not buyers accumulating
