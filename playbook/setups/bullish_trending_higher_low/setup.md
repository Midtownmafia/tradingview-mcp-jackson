# Setup 3B: Bullish Trending Profile Higher Low Continuation
**Mirror of:** `bearish_trending_lower_high` (Setup 3)  
**Type:** Trend Continuation  
**Difficulty:** Beginner/Intermediate — cleaner entry, tighter stop, trend is your friend  
**Instrument:** COMEX_MINI:MGC1! (Micro Gold Futures) — 1 Minute Chart  
**Session:** Any approved kill zone  

---

## Core Concept

Exact mirror of Setup 3. A bullish trending profile means buyers are in control — POC is near the bottom, thin volume above, price is moving up. After the initial move up, price pulls back (normal retracement). That pullback fails to reach the prior low, forming a **higher low** — smart money supporting the dip. Entry is on the break above the higher low candle's high. The LVN above accelerates the next leg.

---

## How to Identify a Bullish Trending Profile

- Volume profile histogram is **skewed/asymmetric** — heavy volume at the BOTTOM, thin volume above
- **NOT a balanced bell curve**
- **POC is in the bottom 30% of the profile range** — most business was done at lower prices
- Price is moving UP and away from the heavy volume node
- The thin area above the POC = the LVN = the fast lane for the next leg up

## Setup Anatomy

### Phase 1 — Initial Move Up + Liquidity Sweep
- Price makes a significant low (liquidity grab / spike below a level)
- Immediately reverses with strong bullish momentum
- Volume on the up bars is heavier than volume on prior down bars
- Trending profile shape begins to form — POC migrating toward the bottom of the range

### Phase 2 — Normal Retracement
- After the initial leg up, price pulls back downward
- **This pullback is the setup forming — watch it carefully**
- Pullback bars should have LOWER volume than the buying bars
- Pullback feels weak — small bodies, wicks on bottoms, no conviction
- The pullback approaches (but does NOT reach) the prior swing low

### Phase 3 — Higher Low Confirmation
- Price stalls above the prior swing low — **higher low is confirmed**
- Higher low coincides with a support level:
  - Prior session VAL
  - Prior POC now acting as support
  - Key horizontal ray (Weekly/Overnight/PDaily level)
- The higher low candle shows: small body, lower wick, close near the high
- 5m and 15m momentum turning bullish again

### Phase 4 — Entry Trigger
- **ENTRY: 1m close ABOVE the higher low candle's high**
- This is the break of the higher low structure — momentum resuming up
- Do NOT enter on the higher low candle itself — wait for the break

### Phase 5 — LVN Acceleration
- Price enters the LVN above — accelerates through thin volume area
- Strong volume bars resume the uptrend
- Target is the next HVN above, then the liquidity pool above that

## Entry, Stop, Target

| Parameter | Level | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| **Entry** | 1m close above the higher low candle's high | Break of structure |
| **Stop (tight)** | Below the higher low wick | 5–10 ticks typically |
| **Stop (standard)** | Below the higher low - 2 ticks buffer | Gives a little room |
| **Target 1** | MTF POC cluster | Partial / move stop to BE |
| **Target 2** | Liquidity pool above (prior highs / VAH cluster) | Full target |
| **Target 3** | Next key level (Weekly/Overnight/PDaily) | Runner |

**R:R note:** Stop is very tight (5–10 ticks below higher low wick). Target is the prior swing high or session VP level above — often 30–80 ticks away. Best R:R of all four setups.

## Alert Criteria (When to Get Ready)

Alert fires when ALL of the following are true:
1. Trending profile detected (POC in bottom 30% of profile range)
2. Price made a prior swing low (visible reference point)
3. Price is currently in a pullback phase (moving back toward prior low)
4. 4H/1H bias is bullish
5. Session more than 30 minutes old

**WATCH state — monitor for higher low to form.**

---

## Entry Criteria (When to Pull the Trigger)

Enter LONG when ALL of the following are true:
1. Price pulled back to within 10–20 ticks of prior swing low BUT closed above it = **higher low confirmed**
2. Higher low coincides with a known support level (prior VAL, POC, or horizontal ray)
3. Higher low candle shows: small body, lower wick, bullish close
4. Pullback volume was noticeably lower than the initial buying volume
5. 5m and 15m momentum turning bullish (bullish bodies forming)
6. Next 1m bar closes above the higher low candle's high = **entry trigger**
7. Clear liquidity pool / target visible above = you know where you're going

---

## Take Profit Criteria

**Partial / Target 1 (MTF POC):**
- Close half at MTF POC
- Move stop to breakeven on remainder

**Full / Target 2 (Liquidity pool / session VP level above):**
- Prior swing highs, VAH cluster, or session VP level above
- Exit if 5m candle closes back below 5m VAL (momentum lost)

**Trail stop rule:**
- Once T1 hit, trail stop to swing low of each pullback
- Exit immediately if price closes below the higher low (structure broken)

---

## Stop Management

- Initial stop: below the higher low wick
- If price immediately accelerates up: trail stop to swing low of each subsequent pullback
- Hard exit: price closes BELOW the prior swing low = higher low structure broken, thesis wrong

---

## Key Observations / Lessons

1. **The tight stop is the edge**: 5–10 ticks below the higher low wick. Small risk, large reward. Do not widen it.

2. **Profile shape is the first filter**: balanced profile = Setup 1, not Setup 3B. Only take this in a trending market.

3. **The higher low must stay above the prior low**: if price takes out the prior low, bullish structure is broken. Exit and reassess.

4. **Volume on the pullback must be lower**: if pullback bars show equal or greater volume than the buying bars, sellers are absorbing — not a healthy retracement.

5. **The LVN above is your tailwind**: once price enters the LVN, it accelerates. Let it run to the next HVN before reassessing.

---

## What Would Have Invalidated This Setup

- Price closes BELOW the prior swing low = structure broken, trend reversing down
- Profile begins to balance (new volume building below POC) = trending shape breaking down
- Pullback forms but has equal/greater volume than buying bars = sellers absorbing
- Higher low forms with no coinciding support level = lower conviction
- 4H candle is indecision / doji — no clear bias
- Kill zone less than 30 minutes old
