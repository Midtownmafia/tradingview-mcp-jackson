## Alert Criteria (When to Get Ready)

Alert fires when ALL of the following are true:
1. Price closes BELOW VAL on 5m timeframe
2. Price closes BELOW VAL on 15m timeframe  
3. Price closes BELOW VAL on 1H timeframe
4. We are more than 30 minutes into the kill zone session
5. At least one bounce attempt has failed to reclaim VAL (churning below)

**This is the WATCH state — not entry yet. Start monitoring for entry trigger.**

## Entry Criteria (When to Pull the Trigger)

Enter SHORT when ALL of the following are true after the alert fires:
1. Price attempted to reclaim VAL at least once and failed (closes back below)
2. The bounce volume was noticeably lower than the selling volume
3. A bearish bar closes at or near its low after the failed bounce
4. Price is making lower highs on each bounce attempt
5. No major key level (Weekly VH/VL, PDaily VH/VL, Overnight VH/VL) sitting within 20 ticks below as immediate support

## Key Observations / Lessons

1. **The liquidity sweep at 9:01pm ET** (spike above kill zone VAH to 4858.5 then reversal) was the first signal that smart money had taken liquidity and was distributing. This is the earliest warning sign.

2. **Volume signature on bounces is the confirmation**: selling bars 227/357/460, bounce bars 222/245/227 — nearly identical volume but price couldn't go up. Sellers were absorbing every bounce.

3. **The LVN below kill zone VAL** caused price to accelerate sharply (795 then 704 volume in consecutive flush bars). This is what creates the 3:1 R:R — the target is further away than the stop because price moves fast through thin areas.

4. **12 bars of churning below VAL** before the move resumed. Patience was required. The alert fires early, the entry is later. Do not rush the entry on the first break.

5. **No key levels were drawn on this chart** — the move may have been even more tradeable if the Overnight VL / PDaily VL were drawn, as they would have identified the target zone in advance.

## What Would Have Invalidated This Setup

- Price closing back ABOVE 5m/15m VAH (4847.20) with strong volume = abort
- Price finding a major key level (Overnight VL, PDaily VL) within 20 ticks of entry = wait
- Kill zone less than 30 minutes old = too early, profile not yet reliable
- All three MTF VP bounces showing INCREASING volume = buyers absorbing, not sellers distributing
